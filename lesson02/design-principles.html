White Space and Clean Design

Apple.com

  • Simple
  • Clean
  • Visual Balance

Apple uses white space really well. They go for the simple and clean look by using a picture and writing only what is necessary next to it. Apple does not over share and over over-complicate their product descriptions

Contrast

Lego.com

  • Visual Interest
  • Highlights Key Elements
  • Readable

Lego uses contrast a lot in their website. They use mostly contrast of color and they use it well. They choose colors that have enough contrast and match/look really good. In this screenshot you can see the contrast of the dark purple photo and the white button. Your eyes are automatically drawn to the button because the color pops out from its environment.

Rule Of Thirds

Nike.com

  • Balance
  • Guides the Eyes
  • Flows

Nike uses the rule of thirds to its advantage. Nike sells sports gear such as shoes and clothes. In the photos showing off their logo and gear, there is almost always the rule of thirds in use. As you can see in this photo, the picture uses the rule of thirds and successfully shows off the happiness of the model and also their logo to advertise.
