About Us

We get all of our fruits from fruit farms that avoid pesticides and produce all organic fruits! It makes our smoothies packed with true fruit flavor! Ask our employees at the pickup location how they feel about organic fruit, here at bountiful foods we ALL rave about organic fruit.

Have you ever had a freshly made fruit smoothie by the ocean? If you have not, then you are in for a treat! Our smoothies taste the best when they see the sunshine! Take a picture if your smoothie by the beach, and we will give you a discount at pickup! We love and appreciate all of our customers, so we will listen to every bit of feedback that you have!
